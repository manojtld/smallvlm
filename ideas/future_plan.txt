Model:

  SFT:
    - First priority is to retain the general language capability of the model
        - 5-10% of the training data needs to be general image/video caption data and high quality
          text reasoning data
        - helps tremendously in the later RL phase since RL relies on the model being able to 
          produce diverse reasoning chains that can be trained with GRPO/GSPO
    - Track and optimize losses separately for reasoning/observation/scoring sections
  
  RL:
    - Monitor KL divergence closely and have tighter constraints
    - Scaling up number of rollouts and the number of rewards
    - Specific reward for guideline following and sound reasoning
    - Research on how to weigh different rewards so it aligns with the final goal of generating
      clincally useful reports
    - Curriculum and efficiency:
        - Schedule the training to go from easy/small reasoning chains -> hard/long reasoning chains
        - Curate data to be heavy on confusing/subtle cases that need guideline based adjudication
    - Large scale synthetic data of preference pairs to train DPO 
        - inexpensive compared to GRPO
        - Can be curated to target exact flaws with the model
